Fruit Crisp

Ingredients

  • 1-2 cups of any fruit
  • 1 cup of oats
  • 1/3 cup of flour
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • Half stick of butter

Instructions

  1. Fill the bottom of a medium sized pie container with any fruit
  2. Mix the oats, flour, sugar, and butter into a small bowl
  3. Dump the mix over the fruit
  4. Cook for 30 minutes at 375 degrees
  5. Serve
